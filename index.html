--- layout: default --- Portugal Crypto Day 2024 will take place in Lisbon, and showcase some of the latest research taking place in the field of cryptography. The event is being organised as part of the CertiCoLab project partnership between FCT NOVA and Simula UiB (Norway).

Date: 13th December 2024

Location: Universidade NOVA de Lisboa, Lisboa, Portugal (exactly where is still TBD)

Cost: Zero! Registration is required for estimating numbers of attendees.

Planned speakers:

  • Manuel Barbosa, FCUP
  • Sofía Celi, Brave Software
  • João Ribeiro, IST
  • Luís Brandão, NIST
  • Manuel Goulão, OIST
  • Mariana Gama, KU Leuven
  • Pedro Branco, MPI-SP

Proposals for short talks: We are inviting proposals for short talks from students and early-career researchers in cryptography, see the Short Talks page for details on how to register a proposal.

Contact email: If you have any questions, please email us at organisers@cryptoday.pt.

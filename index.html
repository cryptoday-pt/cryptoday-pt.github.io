--- layout: default --- Portugal Crypto Day 2024 will take place in Lisbon, and showcase some of the latest research taking place in the field of cryptography. The event is being organised as part of the CertiCoLab project partnership between FCT NOVA and Simula UiB (Norway).

Date: 13th December 2024

Location: Universidade NOVA de Lisboa, Lisboa, Portugal (exactly where is still TBD)

Cost: zero! Registration is required for estimating numbers of attendees, see https://cryptoday.pt/registration/.

Planned speakers (alterations may occur):

  • Manuel Barbosa
  • Sofía Celi
  • João Ribeiro
  • Luís Brandão
  • Manuel Goulão
  • Mariana Gama
  • Pedro Branco

Proposals for short talks: We are inviting proposals for short talks, see https://cryptoday.pt/short-talks/ for more details.
